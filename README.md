## Overview

The Circular City Concept proposes that existing cities can be progressively redesigned — not rebuilt from scratch — to function as circulating systems embedded within nature.

Core transformation axes:

- Water circulation: rainwater harvesting, reclaimed water networks, groundwater recharge
- Heat management: urban cooling through vegetation, water, and soil
- Energy: distributed renewable generation, in-pipe micro-hydropower, building-integrated solar
- Food: urban farming, composting, organic matter return to soil
- Organic matter circulation: closing the loop between city waste and soil regeneration

This concept is closely related to Urban–Civilization OS, and serves as its retrofit-oriented implementation layer.
